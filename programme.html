Programme

You might be pleased to know that we have almost finalised all the arrangements for the day! Below is some info about what will be going on. We’re going to give you a complete programme on the day so that you know exactly what’s happening and where, and we want a few surprises at the wedding, so this is only a short summary!

We’re really looking forward to seeing you then.

Lots of love, Alex and Alex

1.30pm : Wedding ceremony in the Chapel. When you get to Queens’, you will be directed from the Porters’ Lodge across the mathematical bridge to the chapel. It’s nice and easy to find.

2.15pm: Straight after the ceremony there will be food and drinks outside (as long as its sunny!) The afternoon’s activities will be on grass as well as concrete, so you might want to think about this when picking your shoes so that heels don’t sink in the lawn!

5.00pm: We hope that there will be plenty going on in the afternoon to keep you content until we head in to dinner at about 5pm. We’ll be crossing over the mathematical bridge to dine in Cripps Hall.

7.30pm/8:00pm: The after-dinner party will be focused in Old Hall and the Old Kitchens. There’ll be cocktails, music and, of course, dancing! The party in college will finish at 11pm. For the energetic amongst you, a group will be going out in Cambridge afterwards.
